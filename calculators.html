Energy consumption calculation

The energy E in kilowatt-hours (kWh) per day is equal to the power P in watts (W) times number of usage hours per day t divided by 1000 watts per kilowatt:

E(kWh/day)=P(W)×t(h/day) / 1000(W/kW)

Electricity cost calculation

The electricity cost per day in RON(lei) is equal to the energy consumption E in kWh per day times the energy cost of 1 kWh.
Example: 1kWh costs 0,79 lei (in some counties)

Cost(lei/day)=E(kWh/day)×Cost(lei/1kWh)
